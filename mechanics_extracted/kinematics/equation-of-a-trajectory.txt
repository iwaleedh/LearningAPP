Examiner Tips and Tricks
The steps are always the same so the only way the questions
can be made difficult is for them to use confusing letters. They
could use similar letters to suvat including capital letters.
Alternatively, they could use unusual letters like a, instead of θ,
for angles.
Make sure you show enough working out to convince the
examiner that you know the steps especially if it is a "show
that" question.
Sometimes you might have to use trigonometric
identities from pure maths.
