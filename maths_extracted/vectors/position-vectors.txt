Position vectors
What is a position vector?
Position vectors describe the position of a point in
relation to the origin
They are different to displacement vectors which
describe the direction and distance between any two
points

Distance between two points Got it
The distance between two points is the magnitude of the
vector between them (see Magnitude Direction)
