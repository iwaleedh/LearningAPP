How do I integrate f'(x)/f(x)?
“The top is ‘almost’ the derivative of the bottom”
'almost' here meaning 'a multiple of' (see below)
The integral will involve ln |f(x)| - ie ln of the bottom

Examiner Tips and Tricks
If you're unsure if the fraction is of the form f’(x)/f(x),
differentiate the denominator.
Compare this to the numerator but you can ignore any
coefficients.
If the coefficients do not match then ‘adjust’ and
‘compensate’ for them.
