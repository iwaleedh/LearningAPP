Examiner Tips and Tricks
If in doubt you can always use a substitution.
Differentiation is easier than integration so if stuck try the
opposite, eg. sin and cos are linked (remember that
minus!) so if integrating a sin function, start by
differentiating the corresponding cos function.
Lastly, check your final answer by differentiating it.
