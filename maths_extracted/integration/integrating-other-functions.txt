Examiner Tips and Tricks
Make sure you have a copy of the formula booklet during
revision but don't try to remember everything in the
formula booklet.
However, do be familiar with the layout of the formula
booklet – you’ll be able to locate quickly whatever you are
after, and you do not want to be searching every line of
every page!
For formulae you think you have remembered, use the
booklet to double-check.
