What is a general solution?
Integration will be involved in solving the differential
equation
ie working back to “y = f(x)”
A constant of integration, c is produced
This gives an infinite number of solutions to the differential
equation, each of the form y = g(x) + c (ie y = f(x) where
f(x) = g(x) + c)
These are often called a family of solutions …
… and the solution y= g(x) + c is called the general
solution
