What is a recurrence relation?
A recurrence relation describes each term in a sequence
as a function of the previous term – ie u = f(u )

Examiner Tips and Tricks
For arithmetic or geometric sequences defined by
recurrence relations, you can sum the terms using the
arithmetic series and geometric series formulae.
To sum up the terms of other sequences, you may have to
think about the series and find a clever trick.
