Examiner Tips and Tricks
If an exam question asks you to give an answer, for
example, "in the form p + q√3 , where p and q are rational
numbers", this does NOT mean that p and q have to be
integers, or positive!
Remember: both integers and fractions (both positive
and negative) are rational numbers
