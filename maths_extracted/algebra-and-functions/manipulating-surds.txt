What are surds?
If nis a positive integer and is not a square number, then √n
(or any multiple of √n ) is a surd
Stuck on something?

Where can surds be useful?
Using surds lets you leave answers in exact form
e.g. 5 2 rather than 7.071067812
Understanding how to simplify surds can help reduce
expressions and collect like terms
