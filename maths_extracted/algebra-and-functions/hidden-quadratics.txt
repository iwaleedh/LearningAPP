What are hidden quadratic
equations?
Hidden quadratic equations are quadratics written in
terms of a functionf(x)
A normal quadratic appears in the form ax2+bx+c=0
Whereas a hidden quadratic appears in the form
⎡ ⎤ ⎡ ⎤
a⎢ ⎣f(x)⎥ ⎦2+b⎢ ⎣f(x)⎥ ⎦+c=0
This might look complicated but it simply means x has
been replaced by f(x)
e.g. sin2x+2sinx−3=0 is just the hidden quadratic of
x2+2x−3=0 where f(x) =sinx
