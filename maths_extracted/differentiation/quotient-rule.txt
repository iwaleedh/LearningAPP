Examiner Tips and Tricks
The quotient rule formula is in the formulae booklet – you
don't have to memorise it.
Be careful using the formula – because of the minus sign
in the numerator the order of the functions is important.
Look out for functions of the form f(x)=g(x)(h(x))−1
You could differentiate that using a combination of
the chain rule and the product rule (and it can be
good practice for you to try it!)
But it can also be seen as just a quotient rule question
in disguise
