Examiner Tips and Tricks
The formulae for the derivatives of sec x, cosec x, and cot
x are in the formulae booklet – you don't need to
memorise them
However, you should know how to derive the
derivatives of sec, cosec, and cot using the chain rule
The formulae for the derivatives of arcsin, arccos, and
arctan are not in the formulae booklet
You should know how to derive those derivatives
using the reciprocal form of the chain rule
