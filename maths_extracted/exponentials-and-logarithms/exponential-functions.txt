Exponential functions Help me understand
What are exponential functions?
Exponential functions of the form y = ax with a > 0 are
considered at A level
What are exponential graphs?
All graphs of the form y = ax will pass through (0, 1)
because a0 = 1
The x-axis is an asymptote

What is exponential growth?
Got it
Where x < 0 the higher value of a is the “lower” graph
e.g. 3x<2x when x<0
Where x > 0 the higher value of a is the “higher” graph
e.g. 3x>2x when x>0
a > 1 is exponentialgrowth (see Exponential Growth &
Decay)
What is exponential decay?
Where x < 0 the higher value of a is the “lower” graph
e.g. 0.3x<0.2x when x<0
Where x > 0 the higher value of a is the “higher” graph
e.g. 0.3x>0.2x when x>0
0 < a < 1 is exponential decay
You may like to think about why a = 1 is not considered... If
a = 1, y = 1x = 1 for all values of x
