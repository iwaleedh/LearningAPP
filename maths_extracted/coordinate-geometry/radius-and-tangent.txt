Radius & tangent
What is the relationship between a
tangent and a radius?
A tangent is a line that touches a circle at a single point but
doesn't cut across the circle

How do I find the equation of a
tangent to a circle?
STEP 1: Find the gradient of the radius OP
STEP 2: Find the gradient of the tangent
STEP 3: The equation of the tangent is the equation of the
line with that gradient that goes through point P(see
Equation of a Straight Line)
Examiner Tips and Tricks
If you understand the formula in Step 2 above, you can
find the gradient of the tangent without having to find the
gradient of the radius first.
