Equation of a straight line
What is the equation of a straight
line?
y = mx + c is the equation for any straight line
m is gradient given by “difference in y” ÷ “difference in x”
or dy/dx
c is the y-axis intercept
Alternative form is ax + by + c = 0 where a, b and c are
integers
