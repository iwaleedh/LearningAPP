x = g(x) iteration
What is iteration?
When an equation cannot be solved using the usual
analytical methods, we can still find approximate
solutions to a certain degree of accuracy
Iteration is one way to do this, by repeatedly using each
answer as the new starting value for a function, we can
achieve an ever more accurate answer
What does x = g(x ) mean?

What are staircase diagrams and
cobweb diagrams?
Iterations can be shown on diagrams called staircase or
cobweb diagrams
These can be drawn by plotting the graphs of y = x
against y = g(x) from your iterative formula
Examiner Tips and Tricks
You must show all your steps when rearranging an
equation into an iterative formula
Working backwards can often be helpful to figure out
how an equation has been rearranged but you must write
your answer as if you worked forwards
Use ANS button on your calculator to calculate repeated
iterations
