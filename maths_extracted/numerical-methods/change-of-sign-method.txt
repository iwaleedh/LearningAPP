Change of sign method
What does a change of sign mean?
A sign change between f(a) to f(b) means there must be a
root between a and b
When an equation cannot be solved using the usual
analytical methods, we can use change of sign to find
approximate solutions
We can say a root lies within a particular interval or is
correct to a given accuracy
Bounds can be used to show a root is correct
Using sign change to find a root is only appropriate for
continuous functions in a small interval
