Examiner Tips and Tricks
To solve equations with the reciprocal trig functions,
convert them into the regular trig functions and solve in
the usual way.
Don't forget that both tan and cot can be written in terms
of sin and cos (see the Worked Example below).
You will sometimes see csc instead of cosec for
cosecant.
