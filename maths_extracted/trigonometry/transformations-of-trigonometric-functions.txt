Transformations of

What are transformations of
trigonometric functions?
You should already have a good idea about translating,
stretching and reflecting basic functions (see
Transformations of Functions)
The basic principles are exactly the same for transforming
any trigonometric graph
y = n cos x gives a vertical stretch or squash
y = sin nx gives a horizontal stretch or squash
y = tan (x + c) gives a horizontal translation

Examiner Tips and Tricks
Always sketch with a pencil and draw a smooth curve.
When you sketch the transformation of a graph, be sure
to indicate the new coordinates of any points that are
marked on the original graph.
Try to indicate the coordinates of points where the new
graph intersects the axes.
