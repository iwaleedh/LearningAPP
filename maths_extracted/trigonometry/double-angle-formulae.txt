Double angle formulae
What are the double angle
formulae?
Help me understand
The double angle formulae are derived from the 'A+B'
versions of the compound angle formulae:
sin(2A)≡2sinAcosA
cos(2A)≡cos2A−sin2A
cos(2A)≡2cos2A−1
cos(2A)≡1−2sin2A
2tanA
tan(2A)≡
1−tan2A

Examiner Tips and Tricks
The double angle formulae are not included in the formula
booklet – you have to know them.
If you forget them, you can always derive them from the
compound angle formulae (which are in the formula
booklet) as shown above.
